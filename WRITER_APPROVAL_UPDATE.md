# Writer Video Approval Update

## Change
Added a **Script Content** display section to the `WriterVideoApproval` view.

## Details
- Located in `components/writer/WriterVideoApproval.tsx`.
- Displays the full script content (with HTML decoding) above the Video Content section.
- Allows writers to cross-reference the script against the final video during the approval process.

## Visuals
- Styled with standard white box, black border, and shadow.
- Script text uses serif font for readability, similar to other script views.
